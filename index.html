PromptShift

Enhance prompts directly inside ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and other text inputs.

Shortcuts

  1. Focus a text box and type at least 3 characters.
  2. Press Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + E to enhance.
  3. Press Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + Y to hide/show the pill.

If shortcuts stop working after an extension reload, refresh the page tab once.
